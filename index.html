Lebron James

Intro

LeBron James is a professional basketball player in the National Basketball League. He plays small forward currently for the Cleveland Cavaliers. James is currently 31 years old, married, and has two children.

Personal Info

Born December 30, 1984 in Akron, Ohio
Nationality American
Listed Height 6 ft 8 in
Listed Weight 250 lb
NBA Draft 2003/Round:1/Pick:1st Overall

Acheivements

James has won three NBA championships , four NBA Most Valuable Player Awards , three NBA Finals MVP Awards , two Olympic gold medals , an NBA scoring title , and the NBA Rookie of the Year Award. He has also been selected to 12 NBA All-Star teams (named the game's MVP twice), 12 All-NBA teams, and six All-Defensive teams, and is the Cavaliers' all-time leading scorer.

Legacy

Lebron James is one of the worlds most popular athletes and celebrities. He has accumulated extremely large amounts of money through his professional basketball career and his numerous endorsement deals. Some people consider Lebron James to be the “face of the NBA.”
